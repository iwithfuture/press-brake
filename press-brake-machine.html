WE67K vs WC67K

Choose CNC accuracy or economical NC bending

Send thickness & length

WE67K and WC67K are both press brake series, but they serve different buyer needs. The key difference is control accuracy, synchronization method, automation level, and investment budget.

WE67K Series

CNC Press Brake

Best for buyers who need higher bending accuracy, repeat production, multi-step jobs, controller programs, and CNC crowning options.

  • Electro-hydraulic servo synchronization.
  • Better for cabinets, stainless panels, elevator panels, and repeat export parts.
  • Recommended when angle consistency and operator efficiency matter more than lowest purchase cost.
WC67K Series

NC Hydraulic Press Brake

Best for workshops that need reliable hydraulic bending capacity, simpler operation, and a lower investment for common sheet-metal work.

  • Torsion bar hydraulic structure with NC back-gauge control.
  • Suitable for HVAC ducting, metal doors, brackets, furniture parts, and general fabrication.
  • Recommended when the budget is controlled and bending work is less complex.
Quick decision: Choose WE67K for higher precision, repeatability and CNC workflow. Choose WC67K for economical general bending when the part accuracy requirement is not as strict.

CNC crowning compensation

The catalog explains that CNC crowning calculates compensation values from ram and bed surface profiles, helping the machine maintain better conformity during bending.

  • Helps reduce angle variation caused by ram and bed deflection.
  • Supports better product flatness and angular accuracy after bending.
  • Motorized and hydraulic crowning configurations can be discussed based on machine type and accuracy requirement.
